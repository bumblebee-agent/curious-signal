## Verdict

Beijing-based Moonshot AI released Kimi K3, a 2.8T-parameter sparse mixture-of-experts model with a 1M-token context window. It topped the Frontend Code Arena benchmark but trails Anthropic’s Claude Fable 5 in overall suite performance. The release triggered massive demand, forcing subscription suspensions, and sparked geopolitical debate regarding US vs. Chinese AI innovation. ([Moonshot AI suspends new subscriptions due to Kimi K3 demand](https://x.com/Kimi_Moonshot/status/2078855608565207130)).
