# OpenAI Launches ChatGPT Work and Unified Desktop App with GPT-5.6

OpenAI has broadly released GPT-5.6 and the new 'ChatGPT Work' tool, following a staggered rollout requested by the U.S. government. This release coincides with a new unified ChatGPT desktop application for macOS and Windows that integrates Chat, Work, and Codex modes. The standalone browser-based Atlas feature is being discontinued in favor of this new app-centric approach.

## Key Points

- ChatGPT Work is a new mode for research and deliverables, currently rolling out to eligible accounts.
- The new desktop app unifies Chat, Work, and Codex; Codex users must update their existing app.
- GPT-5.6 was released broadly on Thursday, July 9, 2026.
- The rollout timing aligns with shifting U.S. government AI policy under the Trump administration.

## What Is New

- The 'ChatGPT Work' mode and the unified desktop app combining Chat, Work, and Codex. The release of GPT-5.6.

## Why It Matters

It marks a shift from a web-first, single-model interface to a multi-mode, desktop-centric ecosystem. The government-requested rollout highlights the increasing political scrutiny of AI releases.
