# Summary: GPT-Live (OpenAI)

**Overview**
OpenAI has launched **GPT-Live**, a new generation of voice models designed to replace previous ChatGPT Voice modes (Standard and Advanced). It aims to create a more natural, fluid human-AI interaction experience through a full-duplex architecture and background delegation capabilities.

**Key Features & Architecture**
*   **Full-Duplex Architecture:** Unlike previous turn-based models, GPT-Live can listen and speak simultaneously. It can interrupt, pause, or provide active listening cues (e.g., "mhmm," "yeah") in real-time, allowing for natural back-and-forth conversation without rigid turn-taking.
*   **Background Delegation:** GPT-Live handles the continuous interaction layer, while delegating complex tasks (web search, deep reasoning, agentic work) to a frontier model in the background. This allows the voice interface to maintain flow while the backend model processes complex queries.
*   **Underlying Models:**
    *   At launch, GPT-Live uses **GPT-5.5** in the background.
    *   **GPT-Live-1:** Uses GPT-5.5 Instant (fast) or GPT-5.5 Thinking (Medium/High reasoning effort).
    *   **GPT-Live-1 mini:** Optimized for efficiency.
*   **Visual Enhancements:** ChatGPT Voice now displays rich visual cards for topics like weather, stocks, and sports while the user speaks.

**Performance & Benchmarks**
*   **User Preference:** Human evaluations show strong preference for GPT-Live over Advanced Voice Mode regarding conversational flow, turn-taking, and naturalness.
*   **Reasoning & Search:** GPT-Live-1 substantially outperforms Advanced Voice Mode on **GPQA** (expert-level scientific reasoning) and **BrowseComp** (agentic web search).
*   **Task Execution:** Outperforms previous models on **τ³-Voice Telecom** (multi-turn telecom support tasks).

**Safety & Guardrails**
*   **Real-time Intervention:** Safeguards can act while the model is speaking, steering responses, surfacing resources, or ending conversations in high-risk cases.
*   **Specific Risk Areas:** Enhanced testing for self-harm, psychosis, emotional reliance, violence, and sexual content.
*   **Teen Safety:** Age-appropriate behavior training, parental controls, and notifications for linked parents in high-risk situations (e.g., self-harm intent).
*   **Anti-Impersonation:** Designed for conversation, not voice cloning; uses predefined voices with safeguards against imitating real people.
*   **Monitoring:** Long-term measurement focused on emotional reliance and well-being.

**Availability & Constraints**
*   **Rollout:** Global rollout to ChatGPT users on iOS, Android, and ChatGPT.com.
    *   **Go/Plus/Pro Users:** Default is GPT-Live-1.
    *   **Free Users:** Default is GPT-Live-1 mini.
*   **API Access:** Not yet available; developers/enterprises can sign up for notifications.
*   **Language Support:** Optimized for popular languages; some non-native accents or fluency gaps may exist in other languages.
*   **Limitations at Launch:**
    *   Does **not** support voice with video or screen sharing (legacy modes still support this).
    *   Voice capabilities are distinct from video/screen sharing features.

**Implementation Guidance for Operators**
*   **Integration:** Monitor for API availability via the provided sign-up form.
*   **Safety Review:** Review the [GPT-Live System Card](https://deploymentsafety.openai.com/gpt-live) for detailed safety metrics and risk mitigations.
*   **User Expectations:** Inform users that while the experience is more natural, background processing may introduce slight latency for complex queries, though the voice interface remains responsive.
*   **Legacy Fallback:** Users requiring video/screen sharing with voice must use legacy Standard or Advanced Voice Modes.
