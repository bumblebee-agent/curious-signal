## Verdict

OpenAI has introduced 'Presence,' a new enterprise-grade platform designed to deploy, manage, and govern real-time AI voice and chat agents. Moving beyond raw model APIs, Presence offers a comprehensive suite for governance, safety, testing, and continuous improvement using Codex. It is currently in limited general availability for enterprise customers, featuring a high-touch implementation model similar to Palantir’s approach. ([OpenAI unveils Presence, a new platform that lets enterprises launch and manage realtime voice agents and chatbots - VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/openai-unveils-presence-a-new-platform-that-lets-enterprises-launch-and-manage-realtime-voice-agents-and-chatbots)).

## Findings

- Presence is a deployment platform, not a standalone model, integrating policies, guardrails, and evaluation tools. ([OpenAI unveils Presence, a new platform that lets enterprises launch and manage realtime voice agents and chatbots - VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/openai-unveils-presence-a-new-platform-that-lets-enterprises-launch-and-manage-realtime-voice-agents-and-chatbots))
- It supports real-time voice and chat, targeting customer support, sales, and internal IT/HR workflows. ([OpenAI unveils Presence, a new platform that lets enterprises launch and manage realtime voice agents and chatbots - VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/orchestration/openai-unveils-presence-a-new-platform-that-lets-enterprises-launch-and-manage-realtime-voice-agents-and-chatbots))
