  <p class="eyebrow">About</p>
  <h1>Notes worth listening to.</h1>

  Curious Signal is a public shelf for morning briefings and research made from a larger private workflow. Each edition keeps only the part worth sharing: the audio, the written brief, and links to the public sources.

  The raw prompts, local machine paths, job records, private notes, and intermediate files stay out of the public archive. When an audio run is incomplete, the edition says so instead of publishing a fragment.

  This first collection is a small field test. The shape is intentionally simple enough to become an automatic publishing step later.
</section>
